VISION

Strive to be an educational institute of excellence that cultivates passionate, confident, and happy individuals, allowing them to explore their innate abilities and become lifelong learners.


MISSION

“Every child is unique”, whether in terms of their skills, learning preferences, hobbies, personalities, or general demeanour, which Gives us a perspective to comprehend, concentrate our efforts, and attend to the specific needs of the child. Contributes to the development of a stress-free, emotionally secure, and enjoyable learning environment for every kid.


Proud to be Partners of

Building Dreams Foundation (BDF) is a non-profit organization registered under A Section 8 Company under the central government of Republic of India. Our Certificate of Incorporation Number is U85320UR2017NPL007579 dated 14th Feb 2017.BDF promotes the social development of disadvantaged children and communities. Our mission is to serve the most vulnerable populations in India. We focus on Food, Education, Environment and Empowerment. Our goal is to transform students’ lives through the medium of music, art, technology, and academic studies. We prepare students for adult life to become productive members of society, who, in turn, serve as role models to inspire others within their communities. Our core values include: justice, equality, critical thinking, cooperation and respect for people and the environment. These values underlie the culture of BDF.
